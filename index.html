About Our Project

Have you ever wanted to be the very best - like no one ever was? To catch them all is THE real test, and to train them is your cause. You will travel across the land searching far and wide to teach every Pokemon the power that's inside. Pokemon, gotta catch'em all!

Welcome aspiring all aspiring PokeMasters! Have you ever thought: "Hey my Poke-Dex, is cool and all, but why can't we us it to find Pokemon?!" Well, this application was developed to help aspiring Pokemon Go! masters, locate all the first generation Pokemon from around the world.

Using player-generated data and advanced data visualization tools, PokeMasters of all walks of life will be able to find specific Pokemon based upon: 1) Name, 2) Type, 3) Weather conditions, and 4) time of day.

BETA: Plot your own custom "Kanto Journey!" - Plug in your zipcode and the application will plot the best possible route to help you find all First Generation in Pokemon Go!
